So

This year I'm going to build one thing every month.

12 projects.

Why?

I often have ideas but I struggle to follow through and actually make them a reality.

Making this public will help me to be held accountable for completing my ideas.

I want to use this as a way to learn new skills and improve my current skill set.

Also, I want to share my work with you.

Maybe something great will come out of it ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

What will you build?

This is my list in no particular order. Some things may change along the way.

It's not all software. Some of these things are hardware based.

  • Blog platform

    A barebones content management system to write blog posts about my projects.

    This page is part of it! (Although it doesn't have any features yet.)

  • LED dog collar

    An LED, battery powered, multi coloured collar for my dog. So she'll look super stylish at the dog park.

  • Cross stitch pattern generator

    A suggestion from my wife. It will turn a photo into a bunch of coloured squares that can be used as a cross stitch pattern.

  • Bicycle customisation

    I want to customise my bike to include some kind of carrier on the back and some safety lights for night time biking.

  • Tournament management software

    An app that will facilitate team, player and tournament management (for basketball).

  • iOS game

    Haven't yet decided what kind of game to build but I want to refresh my iOS development skills.

  • Rasperry Pi solar powered security camera

    Self explanatory. A security camera that runs off solar power and saves regular backups of footage.

  • Vintage radio restoration

    Taking an old vintage tube radio and replacing the electronics to support digital radio and bluetooth.

  • Arcade/video game

    A clone of an old arcade or video game like Pacman, Tetris or Space Invaders.

  • Torrent downloader

    A self contained box on my home network that will download torrents on demand via either text message or some kind of chat bot. Doubles as a media server.

  • Pantry inventory tracker

    An app that keeps track of what's in my fridge and pantry. Supports barcode scanning.

  • Base pattern draw

    Another suggestion from my wife. A tool that will draw a base pattern given a person's body measurements.
